Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and onion over medium heat until no longer pink; drain. Stir in the tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer, uncovered, for 1 hour.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, and rinse with cold water.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 3 noodles over the meat sauce, and top with half of the ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining meat sauce. Cover with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese, and bake an additional 5 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
